Frequently Asked Questions

We get a lot of questions about Flash Smart. Here are some answers ;)
1. What does Flash Smart actually do?
Flash Smart uses a shuffling algorithm that places cards that a user has frequently gotten incorrect towards the top of the deck. This allows users to view cards that they are struggling with more, allowing them to get the most out of their study sessions.

2. Why should I use Flash Smart?
By having cards that you've frequently missed at the top of your deck, you will be able to practice topics you are struggling with. We've put a lot of effort into our shuffling algorithm to make sure you get the most out of every one of your study sessions.

3. How can I use Flash Smart?
Under the create tab on the nav bar, you can create your own set of flashcards and practice right away! We also have 3 sample decks on some of our favorite topics!

4. Where did the inspiration for a smart flashcard web application come from?
The idea came out of necessity. One of our team members, Daniel, came up with the idea when he was studying flashcards for his Russian class. He realized how much easier studying would be if there was a way that that emphasis could be put on the cards he didn't know as well while he was studying. This marked the beginning of Flash Smart!
